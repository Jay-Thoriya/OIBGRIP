Sushant Singh Rajput

A man who was an Indian actor known for his work in Hindi cinema

Here's a time line of Sushant Singh Rajput Life:

  • 1986 - Sushant Singh Rajput was born on January 21, 1986 in Purnia, Bihar to K. K. Singh and Usha Singh. He has 4 sisters (i.e. Neetu, Meetu, Priyanka & Shweta).
  • 2002 - His family moved to Delhi following his mother's death in 2002 where Rajput completed his schooling for intermediate studies in Kulachi Hansraj Model Schoo
  • 2006 - He secured admission in the Delhi College of Engineering (later renamed Delhi Technological University) to pursue a Bachelor of Engineering degree in mechanical engineering.
  • 2006 - According to Rajput, he did not have any interest in engineering but his family gave him no option which left him dissatisfied. He instead wanted to become an astronaut and later an air force pilot but was also interested in Bollywood, being a fan of Shah Rukh Khan.
  • 2010 - In 2011, Rajput publicly proposed to his Pavitra Rishta co-star Ankita Lokhande during a national broadcast of the dance competition show Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa.
  • 2012 - The couple began dating after their first meeting on the set of Pavitra Rishta, where they had been cast as husband and wife to each other.In 2012, they began living together,
  • 2012 - In a 2021 interview, Lokhande said that Rajput wanted to go ahead in his career and they ended their relationship mutually.
  • 2013-2020 - Now he was come to Bollywood and making films like :2013-Kai Po Che!,Shuddh Desi Romance 2014-PK 2015-Detective Byomkesh , Bakshy! 2016-M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story 2017-Raabta 2018-Welcome to New York 2019-Sonchiriya, Chhichhore, Drive 2020-Dil Bechara
  • 2020 -On 14 June 2020, Indian actor Sushant Singh Rajput was found dead, hanging from the ceiling fan at his home in Bandra, Mumbai, aged 34. The cause of death was ruled to have been suicide and the official postmortem reports concluded Rajput died of asphyxia due to hanging.The Mumbai Police launched an investigation into the death, which was the subject of widespread speculation and rumours.
